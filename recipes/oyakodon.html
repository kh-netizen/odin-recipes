Oyakodon Recipe

Description

This Oyakodon recipe is a quick and easy way you can make an authentic Japanese dish at home. Oyakodon itself is a donburi, or Japanese rice bowl dish, in which chicken, egg, sliced scallion (or sometimes regular onions), and other ingredients are all simmered together in a kind of soup that is made with soy sauce and stock, and then served on top of a large bowl of rice.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Sake
  • Onion
  • Eggs
  • Mitsuba
  • Dashi
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sugar
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Cut the onion, green onions, and chicken and crack the eggs.
  2. Cook the onion in the seasonings.
  3. Add the chicken and cook until the chicken is no longer pink.
  4. Drizzle egg mixture at two separate times. Garnish the green onions on top.
  5. Spoon the finished dish over steamed rice.
