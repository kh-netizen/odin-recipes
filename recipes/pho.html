Pho Recipe

Description

This is an easy Vietnamnese insipired Pho soup that includes rice noodles in a flavorful broth with beef, and topped with fresh herbs and vegatables. Pho is a Vietnamnese noodle soup that's made with broth, rice noodles, meat, and fresh garnishes. Authetic versions of this dish usually takes hours, but if you're looking for a quick version with smiliar qualities then you have come to the right place.

Ingredients

  • Beef broth
  • Yellow onion
  • Fresh ginger
  • Ground coriander
  • Cloves
  • Fish sauce
  • Hoisin sauce
  • Soy sauce
  • Red chili paste
  • Cinnamon
  • Salt and pepper
  • Rice noodles
    • <1i>1/2 lb. sirloin steak
  • Flank steak
  • Brisket
  • Green onions
  • Lime wedges
  • Bean sprouts

Steps

  1. Place a large dry pot over medium heat and add the onion and ginger pieces and cook for 4 minutes.
  2. Add the broth, water, coriander, clove, fish sauce, hoisin sauce, soy sauce, chili garlic paste, cinnamon stick, and a pinch of fresh cracked salt and pepper to the pot. Bring to a slow boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 30 minutes or more.
  3. Prepare the noodles according to the package and set aside.
  4. Ready the garnishes in small bowls.
  5. Add the meat 5 minutes before the broth is done cooking.
  6. Discard the ginger, clove, cinnamon stick and onion pieces from the pot.
  7. Ladle the soup and beef over your rice noodles in a bowl and garnish however you like.
