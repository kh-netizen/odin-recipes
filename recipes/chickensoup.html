Chicken Noodle Soup Recipe

Description

This is a quick, easy, and authentic chicken noodle soup that you can make anytime you want. You can make this for your friends and family when they come over or when the cooler seasons hits, but whatever it is you're sure to receive compliments from the people who eat it.

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Good brand of chicken stock
  • Butter
  • Celery
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Rosemary and sage
  • Red pepper

Steps

  1. Add butter, diced celery and carrots to a large stock pot over medium-high heat. Saut√© for 3 minutes. Add garlic and cook for another 30 seconds.
  2. Add the chicken stock and season to taste with pepper, a tiny bit of rosemary, sage, and crushed red pepper.
  3. Once the soup is boiling add the noodles into the broth and cook until al dente. Remove the pot as soon as the noodles are tender.
  4. Add chicken meat to the broth and taste the broth again for seasonings.
  5. Last but not least serve.
